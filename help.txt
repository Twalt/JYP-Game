~~~~~~~~~~~~JYP Game Help~~~~~~~~~~~~
            Commands List
Movement:
	walk forward        - Moves you forward 1
	move forward        - Moves you forward 1
	walk forward x      - Moves you forward x
	move forward x 	    - Moves you forward x
	turn left           - Orients to the left of what you were facing
	turn right          - Orients to the right of what you were facing
	turn around	        - Orients to the opposite direction of what you were facing

Information:
	look left           - Shows anything to your left
	look right          - Shows anything to your right
	compass             - Reveals your Map Orientation
	
	quit                - Exit the game
